welcome to foab solutions

Greetings! We have been established with the major goal of managing the biodegradable household wastes generated in a household as well as commercial setting. The company has been registered as a start up incubator in the Startups Valley, TBI, Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirapally. In the following pages you will get to know more about our novel product, the "Geebin" and also our florist quality pot plants venture, Signature Grow for decorating your office premises and homes.

waste Management

The problem of waste is becoming a serious concern for mankind as urbanization grips the booming cities of India. The waste management crisis should be tackled wisely and should receive fresh impetus.

We can no longer dump the waste materials anywhere.Treating the household organic waste at source can effectively solve the problem of solid wastes.

We present ourselves as a specialist, research-oriented, ornamental plant production people, who grow a wide range of Florist quality pot plants. Our major interests are Succulents, Ferns and Foliages. Our plants are grown in one hundred percent soil and artificial manure free media, clean, non-staining and safe. We use unique and sustainable natural materials as growing substrate and each plant we grow and nurture are marked with nature’s perfect Signature.
